Activities

Recruitment Activities

Seven carefully designed formats — ER handles the location and logistics so you can focus entirely on finding the right talent.

Network Drink

17:00 – 19:00 ~100 students No CV Selection

A daily social drink — the largest student audience of the event. Place a stand, show your materials, and mingle freely with students and other recruiters in a relaxed setting.

Workshop

3 hours 15–20 students CV Selection

Pre-selected students solve a business case or play a game you design. Perfect for assessing analytical ability, teamwork, and individual qualities in action.

Power Networking

2–3 hours ≤ 40 students CV Selection

Four companies from the same sector rotate through speed-networking rounds with pre-selected students. Maximum reach, low-pressure format.

+€400 (Pkg A) · +€500 (Pkg B)

Branch Dinner

From 19:00 15–20 students CV Selection

A 3–4 course dinner shared with 3–4 sector companies. Students rotate tables between courses — every company connects with every student throughout the evening.

Individual Interviews

30 min each 9 students CV Selection

One-on-one sessions with 9 pre-selected students. Ideal for orientation or as a first selection round. Keep open slots for talent you discover during the event.

Informal Recruitment

2–3 hours ≤ 20 students CV Selection

Fully customisable — high tea, sports clinic, escape room. Leave a unique impression in a format that reflects your company culture.

+€250 (activity costs up to €750 included)
Package A Only

Company Dinner

From 19:00 15 students CV Selection

An exclusive dinner for your company alone. ER handles venue and logistics — you focus entirely on building relationships with your 15 hand-picked students.
